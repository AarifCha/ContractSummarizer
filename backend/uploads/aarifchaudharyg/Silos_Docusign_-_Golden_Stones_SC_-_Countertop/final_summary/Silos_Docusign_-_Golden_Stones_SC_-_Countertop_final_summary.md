## 1. Top Issues & Executive Summary
This Subcontract presents significant risks to the Subcontractor, Golden Stones LP, particularly regarding payment certainty, extensive indemnification obligations, and stringent schedule adherence. The contract contains explicit "pay-if-paid" and "condition precedent" clauses, shifting the risk of Owner non-payment entirely to the Subcontractor. Indemnity provisions are broad, requiring defense and indemnification for claims arising from the work, including employee injury and the Contractor's own negligence in certain contexts. "Time is of the essence" language, coupled with short notice periods for delay claims (two days) and potential liquidated damages pass-through from the Prime Contract, creates high schedule pressure. The Contractor has extensive rights to backcharge and offset payments for various reasons, including non-compliance, cleanup, safety violations, and performance issues. Additionally, the Prime Contract is fully incorporated, binding the Subcontractor to terms they may not have fully reviewed, and mandating the use of Procore Pay for all financial documentation. Insurance requirements are detailed and demanding, requiring specific limits, additional insureds, and waivers of subrogation.
